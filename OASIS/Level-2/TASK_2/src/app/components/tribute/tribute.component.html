1992 - Present

"Music is not just what it sounds like; it's how it touches your soul."

Biography

Palak Muchhal is an Indian playback singer celebrated for her philanthropic efforts and her melodious voice. She rose to fame with her songs in Aashiqui 2, such as Chahun Main Ya Naa and Meri Aashiqui. Through her music, she has helped fund life-saving surgeries for underprivileged children, making her a beacon of hope and compassion.
